Video Profile SMPN 5 Kerinci Kanan

Category: Video

About The Project

Every school has a unique story, spirit, and vision deserving to be shared. We had the honor of translating the narrative of SMPN 5 Kerinci Kanan into a comprehensive and heartfelt video profile. Our team was fully responsible for the entire end-to-end production process. This began with conceptualization and scriptwriting, born from the school's core values, moved into production where we captured authentic moments, and concluded with post-production, where every scene was woven into a complete story. Our focus wasn't just on the physical facilities, but on the human interactions—the students' spirit, the teachers' dedication, and the supportive learning atmosphere. The result is a visual piece that is not only informative but also inspirational, serving as a window for the community to see the great potential within SMPN 5 Kerinci Kanan..
